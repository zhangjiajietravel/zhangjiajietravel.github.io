Pet-Friendly Hotels in Zhangjiajie for Travelers with Pets

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Zhangjiajie, with its towering sandstone pillars, lush forests, and breathtaking landscapes, is a dream destination for nature lovers. But what if you’re traveling with your furry friend? Fortunately, Zhangjiajie has embraced the pet-friendly travel trend, offering several accommodations where both you and your pet can enjoy a comfortable stay. Whether you’re exploring the Avatar-inspired peaks of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park or wandering through the glass bridges of Grand Canyon, these pet-friendly hotels ensure no family member gets left behind.

Why Choose a Pet-Friendly Hotel in Zhangjiajie?

Traveling with pets requires extra planning, especially in a destination known for its rugged terrain and outdoor adventures. Pet-friendly hotels in Zhangjiajie provide amenities like pet beds, designated walking areas, and even pet-sitting services, making your trip smoother. Plus, many of these hotels are strategically located near scenic spots, so you won’t miss out on the best views while keeping your pet happy.

Benefits of Staying in Pet-Friendly Accommodations

  • No Separation Anxiety – Your pet stays with you, reducing stress for both of you.
  • Convenient Amenities – Some hotels offer pet bowls, treats, and grooming services.
  • Access to Nature – Many properties have open spaces perfect for walks and playtime.

Top Pet-Friendly Hotels in Zhangjiajie

Here are some of the best hotels that welcome pets, ensuring a memorable stay for you and your four-legged companion.

1. Pullman Zhangjiajie

Located in the heart of the city, Pullman Zhangjiajie is a luxury option that caters to pet owners. The hotel allows small to medium-sized dogs and provides pet-friendly rooms with easy access to outdoor areas.

Pet Policies & Amenities

  • Pet fee: A small additional charge per stay.
  • Designated walking zones: Nearby parks for exercise.
  • In-room amenities: Pet beds and bowls available upon request.

2. Zhangjiajie Minnan International Hotel

This hotel combines modern comfort with pet-friendly policies, making it ideal for travelers exploring Zhangjiajie’s attractions. The Minnan International Hotel is close to Tianmen Mountain, so you and your pet can enjoy stunning views together.

What Makes It Great for Pets?

  • No size restrictions: Welcomes larger breeds.
  • Pet-sitting services: Available for an extra fee.
  • Nearby trails: Easy access to pet-friendly walking paths.

3. The Grand Hotel Zhangjiajie

For those seeking a blend of elegance and pet-friendly hospitality, The Grand Hotel Zhangjiajie is a fantastic choice. The hotel’s spacious grounds allow pets to roam freely, and the staff is known for their warm welcome to furry guests.

Highlights

  • Pet-friendly dining: Outdoor seating where pets can join.
  • Grooming services: On request.
  • Proximity to attractions: Short drive to Zhangjiajie National Forest Park.

4. Holiday Inn Express Zhangjiajie

Budget-conscious travelers will appreciate the Holiday Inn Express Zhangjiajie, which offers a no-fuss, pet-friendly stay. While simpler than luxury options, it provides all the essentials for a comfortable trip with your pet.

Key Features

  • Affordable pet fee: Lower than many competitors.
  • Basic amenities: Food bowls and waste bags provided.
  • Central location: Easy access to public transport and pet-friendly cafes.

Tips for Traveling with Pets in Zhangjiajie

Before booking your stay, keep these tips in mind to ensure a smooth experience for you and your pet.

1. Check Hotel Policies in Advance

Not all pet-friendly hotels have the same rules. Some may restrict certain breeds or sizes, while others charge extra fees. Always confirm before booking.

2. Pack Pet Essentials

Bring familiar items like your pet’s favorite toy, food, and a travel bed to help them feel at home.

3. Explore Pet-Friendly Attractions

While not all parks allow pets, some scenic areas like Baofeng Lake have pet-friendly zones. Research beforehand to avoid disappointment.

4. Keep Your Pet Leashed in Public

Zhangjiajie’s natural beauty can be overwhelming for pets. Keeping them leashed ensures their safety and respects local regulations.

Final Thoughts

Zhangjiajie’s pet-friendly hotels make it possible to share the adventure with your beloved pet. From luxury stays at Pullman Zhangjiajie to budget-friendly options like Holiday Inn Express, there’s something for every traveler. With proper planning, you and your furry companion can enjoy the wonders of Zhangjiajie together—creating unforgettable memories along the way.
