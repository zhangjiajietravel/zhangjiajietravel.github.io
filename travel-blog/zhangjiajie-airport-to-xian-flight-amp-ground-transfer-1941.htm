Zhangjiajie Airport to Xi’an: Flight & Ground Transfer

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Traveling from Zhangjiajie to Xi’an is a journey that bridges two of China’s most iconic destinations. Whether you’re drawn to the towering sandstone pillars of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park or the ancient wonders of Xi’an, this route offers a seamless blend of natural beauty and cultural richness. Here’s everything you need to know about flights, ground transfers, and insider tips to make your trip unforgettable.

Flight Options from Zhangjiajie to Xi’an

Direct Flights

Several airlines operate direct flights between Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport (DYG) and Xi’an Xianyang International Airport (XIY). The flight duration is approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, making it a convenient option for travelers. Major carriers like China Southern Airlines and Hainan Airlines frequently service this route.

  • Frequency: Daily flights are available, with increased options during peak travel seasons.
  • Best Time to Book: To secure the lowest fares, book at least 4–6 weeks in advance. Midweek flights (Tuesday–Thursday) are often cheaper.

Connecting Flights

If direct flights are unavailable or overbooked, consider connecting through major hubs like Beijing, Shanghai, or Chengdu. While this extends travel time (4–6 hours total), it can sometimes offer cost savings or flexible departure times.

Ground Transfer at Zhangjiajie Airport

Getting to the Airport

Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport is located about 10 km from downtown Zhangjiajie. Options to reach the airport include:

  • Taxi: A 20-minute ride costing around ¥50–¥80.
  • Airport Shuttle Bus: Affordable (¥15–¥20) but less frequent. Check schedules in advance.
  • Private Transfers: Many hotels offer shuttle services for guests.

Airport Facilities

Zhangjiajie Airport is small but efficient, with:
- Free Wi-Fi
- Basic dining options (local snacks and coffee)
- Limited duty-free shopping

Pro tip: Arrive 2 hours before departure to account for security checks, especially during holidays.

Arrival in Xi’an: Ground Transfer Options

From Xi’an Xianyang Airport to the City Center

Xi’an’s airport is 40 km northwest of the city. Here’s how to get downtown:

Metro (Subway)

  • Line 14: Connects the airport to Xi’an North Railway Station in 30 minutes.
  • Fare: ¥7–¥10.
  • Hours: 6:00 AM–10:30 PM.

Airport Buses

  • Cost: ¥25–¥30 per person.
  • Routes: Multiple lines serve key areas like the Bell Tower and Xi’an Railway Station.

Taxi or Ride-Hailing

  • Duration: 45–60 minutes, depending on traffic.
  • Cost: ¥120–¥150. Avoid unlicensed taxis; use Didi (China’s Uber equivalent) for safety.

Private Transfers

Pre-booked cars (¥200–¥300) are ideal for groups or late-night arrivals.

Exploring Xi’an: Must-Visit Attractions

The Terracotta Warriors

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this army of clay soldiers is a must-see. Hire a guide to uncover its fascinating history.

Xi’an City Wall

Rent a bike and cycle along this 14th-century wall for panoramic views of the city.

Muslim Quarter

A food lover’s paradise—try yangrou paomo (crumbled bread in lamb soup) and roujiamo (Chinese burgers).

Travel Tips for a Smooth Journey

Best Time to Visit

  • Zhangjiajie: Spring (April–June) and autumn (September–November) for mild weather.
  • Xi’an: Avoid summer (July–August) due to heat; spring and fall are ideal.

Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes (both cities involve lots of exploration).
  • Layers for variable mountain weather in Zhangjiajie.
  • A VPN if you rely on Google services (they’re restricted in China).

Cultural Etiquette

  • Bargain politely at markets.
  • Tipping isn’t customary but appreciated for exceptional service.

Hidden Gems Along the Way

Zhangjiajie’s Lesser-Known Trails

Skip the crowds at Yuanjiajie and explore Tianzi Mountain’s quieter paths.

Xi’an’s Ancient Pagodas

Visit the Small Wild Goose Pagoda for a serene escape from the city bustle.

With this guide, your trip from Zhangjiajie to Xi’an will be as smooth as the flight itself. Safe travels and enjoy the adventure!
