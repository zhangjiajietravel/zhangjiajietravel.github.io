Top Resorts Near Zhangjiajie’s Phoenix Ancient Town

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Nestled in the heart of Hunan Province, Phoenix Ancient Town (Fenghuang Guzhen) is a mesmerizing destination that blends rich history, stunning architecture, and breathtaking natural scenery. While the ancient town itself is a must-visit, the surrounding area boasts some of the most luxurious and serene resorts perfect for travelers looking to unwind after exploring the cobblestone streets and towering karst landscapes. Whether you’re seeking a tranquil retreat or an adventure-packed getaway, these top resorts near Phoenix Ancient Town offer the perfect blend of comfort and excitement.

Luxurious Stays with Stunning Views

1. Pullman Zhangjiajie

Located just a short drive from Phoenix Ancient Town, the Pullman Zhangjiajie is a five-star resort that combines modern elegance with the region’s natural beauty. The resort features spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Guests can enjoy a world-class spa, an infinity pool, and gourmet dining options that highlight local Hunan cuisine.

Why Stay Here?
- Proximity to both Phoenix Ancient Town and Zhangjiajie National Forest Park.
- Rooftop bar with sunset views over the karst formations.
- Exclusive guided tours to nearby attractions.

2. The Grand Hotel Phoenix

For those who prefer staying closer to the ancient town, The Grand Hotel Phoenix offers a blend of traditional charm and contemporary luxury. The architecture reflects the Ming and Qing dynasty styles, with intricately carved wooden beams and serene courtyard gardens. The hotel’s riverside location provides a peaceful escape while keeping you within walking distance of Fenghuang’s bustling night markets.

Highlights:
- Private balconies overlooking the Tuojiang River.
- Authentic local dishes served in an elegant setting.
- Nighttime boat tours arranged directly through the hotel.

Eco-Friendly Retreats for Nature Lovers

3. Zhangjiajie Earthly Paradise Resort

If sustainability is a priority, the Zhangjiajie Earthly Paradise Resort is an ideal choice. Tucked away in a lush valley, this eco-resort emphasizes harmony with nature. The villas are built using locally sourced materials, and the resort operates on solar energy. Guests can participate in organic farming activities or take guided hikes through untouched forests.

Unique Features:
- Treehouse accommodations with glass ceilings for stargazing.
- Farm-to-table dining with ingredients harvested on-site.
- Yoga sessions held in a bamboo pavilion overlooking the mountains.

4. Tianmen Mountain Lodge

For adventurers who want to stay near both Phoenix Ancient Town and Tianmen Mountain, this lodge offers rugged luxury. The design incorporates stone and wood elements, blending seamlessly into the dramatic landscape. The lodge organizes thrilling activities like paragliding and via ferrata climbs for adrenaline junkies.

Don’t Miss:
- The glass walkway extending over a sheer cliff face.
- Hot spring baths with mineral-rich waters.
- Helicopter tours over Zhangjiajie’s iconic peaks.

Boutique Resorts with Cultural Flair

5. Miao Village Retreat

Immerse yourself in the local Miao culture at this intimate boutique resort. The property is designed like a traditional Miao village, with stilted wooden houses and handwoven textiles adorning the interiors. Guests can learn traditional crafts, enjoy folk performances, and even participate in a Miao-style banquet.

Cultural Experiences:
- Handicraft workshops led by local artisans.
- Evening bonfires with ethnic music and dance.
- Guided visits to nearby Miao communities.

6. The Silk Road Inn

Inspired by the historic trade routes, The Silk Road Inn offers a unique thematic stay. Each room is decorated to represent a different region along the ancient Silk Road, from Central Asian motifs to Persian-inspired lounges. The resort also features a museum-like gallery showcasing artifacts from the Silk Road era.

Standout Amenities:
- A rooftop tea house serving rare Chinese and Central Asian blends.
- Calligraphy and tea ceremony classes.
- Customized itineraries focusing on historical sites around Hunan.

Family-Friendly Resorts with Fun for All Ages

7. Happy Valley Resort

Perfect for families, Happy Valley Resort offers a mix of relaxation and entertainment. The resort has a water park, a kids’ adventure zone, and even a mini-zoo with native wildlife. Parents can unwind at the spa while children enjoy supervised activities.

Family Perks:
- Themed family suites with bunk beds and play areas.
- Outdoor movie nights under the stars.
- Easy access to kid-friendly attractions like Baofeng Lake.

8. Zhangjiajie Wonderland Resort

This whimsical resort is designed to feel like a fairy tale, with colorful cottages and enchanted forest trails. The resort organizes treasure hunts, puppet shows, and storytelling sessions to keep young travelers engaged.

Magical Touches:
- A “Dragon’s Lair” playground with slides and climbing nets.
- Costumed characters greeting guests at breakfast.
- Nightly firefly tours in the summer months.

Romantic Getaways for Couples

9. Moonlit Waters Villa

For couples seeking seclusion, Moonlit Waters Villa offers private villas with plunge pools and open-air jacuzzis. The resort specializes in romantic packages, including candlelit dinners on floating pavilions and sunrise hot air balloon rides.

Romantic Add-Ons:
- Couples’ massage sessions in a cliffside cabana.
- Personalized love lock ceremonies by the river.
- Photography services to capture your moments.

10. The Hidden Garden Resort

Surrounded by blooming gardens year-round, this resort is a floral paradise. The scent of osmanthus and peonies fills the air, and the resort’s chefs incorporate edible flowers into their dishes. Private gazebos and hidden pathways make it feel like your own secret garden.

Blooming Highlights:
- Floral bath rituals with rose and jasmine petals.
- Picnic baskets prepared for secluded garden spots.
- Seasonal flower festivals with live music.

Adventure Resorts for Thrill-Seekers

11. Cliffhanger Lodge

True to its name, Cliffhanger Lodge is perched on the edge of a precipice, offering heart-stopping views. The resort caters to extreme sports enthusiasts, with rock climbing guides, white-water rafting trips, and base jumping experiences (for the truly daring).

Adrenaline-Pumping Options:
- Overnight camping on a suspended platform.
- Zip-lining across gorges.
- Survival skills workshops with expert instructors.

12. Cloud Riders Resort

Located at a high altitude, this resort is a hub for paragliding and hang gliding. The rooms are designed like futuristic pods with 360-degree views of the clouds drifting past the mountains.

Sky-High Adventures:
- Tandem flights with professional pilots.
- A “Cloud Walk” suspension bridge.
- Astronomy nights with high-powered telescopes.

Whether you’re drawn to luxury, culture, nature, or adventure, the resorts near Phoenix Ancient Town provide unforgettable experiences tailored to every traveler’s dream. From waking up to misty mountain vistas to falling asleep to the gentle sounds of the Tuojiang River, these stays will elevate your journey through one of China’s most enchanting regions.
