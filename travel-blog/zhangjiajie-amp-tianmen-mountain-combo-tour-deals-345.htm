Zhangjiajie & Tianmen Mountain Combo Tour Deals

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

If you're craving an otherworldly travel experience that blends jaw-dropping natural wonders with adrenaline-pumping adventures, look no further than the Zhangjiajie & Tianmen Mountain Combo Tour. Nestled in China’s Hunan Province, these two iconic destinations offer a surreal escape into landscapes that seem ripped straight from a fantasy novel. Whether you're a nature lover, a photography enthusiast, or an adventure seeker, this combo tour is a must-add to your bucket list.

Why Choose a Zhangjiajie & Tianmen Mountain Combo Tour?

Combining Zhangjiajie National Forest Park and Tianmen Mountain into a single trip isn’t just convenient—it’s a game-changer. Here’s why:

1. Unmatched Scenic Diversity

Zhangjiajie’s towering sandstone pillars, famously inspiring Avatar’s Hallelujah Mountains, contrast beautifully with Tianmen Mountain’s dramatic cliffs, skywalks, and the legendary "Heaven’s Gate."

2. Cost-Effective Travel

Bundling these destinations saves time and money. Many tour operators offer discounted packages covering transport, guided hikes, and even skip-the-line access to popular attractions.

3. Adrenaline & Relaxation in One Trip

From Tianmen’s glass skywalk to Zhangjiajie’s serene river cruises, this combo balances thrill and tranquility perfectly.

Top Attractions You Can’t Miss

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Avatar Hallelujah Mountain

The park’s star attraction, these quartz-sandstone pillars shrouded in mist, look like something from another planet. The best viewpoints? Yuanjiajie Scenic Area and Tianzi Mountain.

Golden Whip Stream

A leisurely 7.5km hike along crystal-clear waters, flanked by lush forests and wildlife like macaques. Perfect for families or those seeking a quieter experience.

Bailong Elevator

Dubbed the "World’s Highest Outdoor Elevator," this 326-meter glass ride offers vertigo-inducing views of the park.

Tianmen Mountain

Tianmen Cave (Heaven’s Gate)

This natural arch, carved into the mountain at 1,300m above sea level, is accessed via the 999-Step Stairway—a challenge worth every step.

Glass Skywalk

Not for the faint-hearted! This cliff-hugging walkway offers unobstructed views of the abyss below.

Tianmen Shan Cableway

One of the world’s longest cableways, this 7.5km ride delivers panoramic vistas of Hunan’s karst landscapes.

Best Combo Tour Deals to Consider

1. 4-Day Classic Combo Tour

  • Highlights: Avatar Mountains, Tianmen Cave, glass skywalk, and a sunset hike in Zhangjiajie.
  • Inclusions: 3-star hotels, park tickets, English-speaking guide.
  • Price Range: $400–$600 per person.

2. 5-Day Luxury Adventure

  • Highlights: Private transfers, VIP cableway access, a night at a cliffside hotel.
  • Inclusions: 4-star lodging, gourmet meals, helicopter tour over Zhangjiajie.
  • Price Range: $900–$1,200 per person.

3. Budget-Friendly 3-Day Express

  • Highlights: Key landmarks with efficient timing.
  • Inclusions: Hostel stays, public transport passes.
  • Price Range: $250–$350 per person.

Pro Tips for Your Trip

  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (April–June) and autumn (September–November) for mild weather and fewer crowds.
  • Packing Essentials: Sturdy hiking shoes, rain gear (mist is common), and a camera with extra batteries.
  • Avoid Peak Season: July–August sees heavy tourist traffic and higher prices.

Hidden Gems & Local Experiences

Fenghuang Ancient Town

Many combo tours include a stop at this 300-year-old riverside town, where cobbled streets and stilt houses ooze old-world charm.

Tujia Minority Culture

Don’t miss a traditional Tujia dance performance or a meal of savory腊肉 (là ròu, cured pork)—a local delicacy.

Nighttime Light Shows

In Zhangjiajie, the "Charming Xiangxi" cultural show blends folklore with dazzling visuals—a perfect post-hike wind-down.

How to Book Your Dream Combo Tour

  1. Compare Packages: Sites like Viator, Klook, and local operators like China Highlights offer varied deals.
  2. Check Reviews: Prioritize tours with high ratings for guides and logistics.
  3. Customize: Many agencies let you tweak itineraries—add a day in Fenghuang or opt for private tours.

Final Thoughts

The Zhangjiajie & Tianmen Mountain Combo Tour isn’t just a trip; it’s a plunge into nature’s most theatrical masterpieces. Whether you’re scaling the stairway to Heaven’s Gate or drifting through Avatar’s floating peaks, every moment feels epic. With the right tour deal, you’ll unlock these wonders seamlessly—leaving you free to focus on the awe, not the logistics.
