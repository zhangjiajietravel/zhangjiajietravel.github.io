Zhangjiajie Resorts with the Best Private Helicopter Transfers

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Few destinations blend natural grandeur with luxury as seamlessly as Zhangjiajie. Known for its towering sandstone pillars, misty valleys, and UNESCO-listed Wulingyuan Scenic Area, this Chinese gem has become a bucket-list staple for adventurers and luxury travelers alike. But what if you could skip the crowded trails and experience Zhangjiajie’s breathtaking landscapes from above? Enter private helicopter transfers—the ultimate way to elevate your stay. Here’s a curated list of resorts offering the best airborne access to this otherworldly destination.

Why Choose a Helicopter Transfer in Zhangjiajie?

Unmatched Views

Zhangjiajie’s Avatar-inspired peaks are even more mesmerizing from the sky. A helicopter ride offers unobstructed panoramas of the "Floating Mountains," deep ravines, and winding rivers—perspectives impossible to replicate on foot.

Time Efficiency

With limited vacation days, why spend hours hiking or waiting for shuttle buses? A 15-minute helicopter transfer can replace a half-day journey, giving you more time to relax at your resort or explore hidden gems.

Exclusive Experiences

Private transfers often include bespoke routes, allowing you to hover over lesser-known formations like Tianmen Mountain’s "Heaven’s Gate" or the glass-bottomed Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Bridge.

Top Resorts with Helicopter Transfers

1. Pullman Zhangjiajie

Nestled at the edge of Wulingyuan, this 5-star resort combines modern luxury with easy access to nature. Their helicopter partnership includes:
- Direct transfers from Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport to the resort’s private helipad (20 minutes vs. 1.5-hour drive).
- Custom scenic flights over the Yuanjiajie "Avatar" peaks at sunset.
- VIP packages with champagne service mid-flight.

2. The Luxe Hotel Tianmen Shanzhuang

Overlooking Tianmen Mountain, this boutique property specializes in exclusivity. Highlights:
- Door-to-summit transfers: Skip the 999-step climb to Tianmen Cave—land directly on the mountain’s summit helipad.
- Night flights for stargazing above the karst formations.

3. Sheraton Zhangjiajie

Ideal for business travelers and families, the Sheraton offers:
- Group charters for up to 6 passengers, perfect for splitting costs.
- Combined tours: A helicopter drop-off at Yellow Dragon Cave, followed by a guided exploration.

Helicopter Tour Add-Ons to Consider

The "Avatar Adventure"

Fly over the Southern Sky Column (renamed "Hallelujah Mountain" for Avatar), then land for a private guided hike through the less-crowded Golden Whip Stream.

Romantic Escape Packages

Resorts like Pullman offer "Proposal Flights" with rose-petal landings and in-air photography.

Photography-Focused Flights

Early-morning slots with open-door seating (safety harnessed) for unobstructed shots of the sea of clouds.

Booking Tips

  • Best season: April–October for clear skies, though winter flights offer dramatic misty vistas.
  • Weight limits: Typically 220 lbs (100 kg) per passenger; inquire about group weight distribution.
  • Safety: Ensure operators are CAAC-licensed and pilots have 1,000+ hours of mountain-flying experience.

The Future of Luxury Travel in Zhangjiajie

With new helipads under construction near Glass Bridge and Baofeng Lake, the era of helicopter tourism is just taking off. Resorts are also experimenting with hybrid packages—think helicopter-assisted sunrise yoga on secluded plateaus or aerial picnics above the clouds.

Whether you’re a thrill-seeker, a honeymooner, or simply short on time, pairing a luxury resort with a private helicopter transfer transforms a visit to Zhangjiajie into a once-in-a-lifetime saga. The only question left: Which vista will you conquer first?
