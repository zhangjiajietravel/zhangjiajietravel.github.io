Zhangjiajie Golf Courses: Where to Play in 2024

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Nestled among the towering sandstone pillars and lush greenery of Hunan Province, Zhangjiajie is a dream destination for travelers seeking adventure and natural beauty. While the national park often steals the spotlight, the region’s golf courses offer a unique way to experience the landscape. In 2024, golf enthusiasts can tee off against breathtaking backdrops, combining sport with unforgettable scenery. Here’s your guide to the best golf courses in Zhangjiajie and why they’re worth adding to your itinerary.

The Rise of Golf Tourism in Zhangjiajie

Golf might not be the first activity that comes to mind when planning a trip to Zhangjiajie, but the sport is gaining traction among luxury travelers and outdoor enthusiasts. The region’s dramatic terrain provides a challenging yet visually stunning setting for golfers. With world-class facilities and a growing number of international visitors, Zhangjiajie’s golf scene is poised to become a hidden gem in Asia’s golfing circuit.

Why Golf in Zhangjiajie?

  • Unique Landscapes: Few places on Earth offer fairways surrounded by karst formations and misty valleys.
  • Year-Round Play: The mild climate allows for golfing almost any time of year.
  • Luxury Amenities: High-end resorts and spas cater to golfers looking for relaxation after a round.

Top Golf Courses to Play in 2024

1. Zhangjiajie Tianmen Mountain Golf Club

Location: Near Tianmen Mountain, about 10 km from downtown Zhangjiajie.

This 18-hole championship course is one of the most prestigious in the region. Designed to blend seamlessly with the natural terrain, it offers panoramic views of Tianmen Mountain’s famous "Heaven’s Gate." The course challenges players with elevation changes and strategically placed hazards, making it a favorite among seasoned golfers.

Highlights:
- Signature holes with cliffside drops.
- A clubhouse featuring local architectural styles.
- Proximity to Tianmen Mountain’s cable car for post-game sightseeing.

2. Suoxiyu International Golf Resort

Location: Within the Suoxiyu Nature Reserve, part of the Wulingyuan Scenic Area.

Surrounded by UNESCO-listed landscapes, this course is a masterpiece of design. The fairways weave through forests and alongside crystal-clear streams, creating a serene yet demanding playing experience. The resort also offers villas and fine dining, making it ideal for a golf-centric getaway.

Highlights:
- Eco-friendly course maintenance practices.
- Night golfing options under LED lighting.
- Easy access to Zhangjiajie’s glass bridge and Avatar filming sites.

3. Zhangjiajie Sunshine Golf Club

Location: A short drive from the city center, near the airport.

Perfect for travelers with limited time, this 9-hole course provides a quick but satisfying round. The layout is beginner-friendly, with wide fairways and minimal water hazards. It’s a great option for families or those new to the sport.

Highlights:
- Affordable green fees compared to larger courses.
- On-site instructors for lessons.
- A relaxed atmosphere with fewer crowds.

Golfing Tips for Zhangjiajie Visitors

Best Time to Play

Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) offer the most comfortable weather. Summers can be humid, while winters are mild but occasionally foggy.

Booking Tee Times

Reservations are recommended, especially at Tianmen Mountain and Suoxiyu. Many resorts offer packages that include accommodations and golf fees.

What to Bring

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing.
  • Extra golf balls (the rough can be unforgiving).
  • A camera—you’ll want to capture the views.

Beyond Golf: Exploring Zhangjiajie

A golf trip here isn’t complete without experiencing the region’s other attractions:
- Zhangjiajie National Forest Park: Hike among the "Avatar Mountains."
- Grand Canyon Glass Bridge: Test your nerves on the world’s longest glass-bottomed bridge.
- Fenghuang Ancient Town: A picturesque detour for culture lovers.

Whether you’re a serious golfer or just looking for a unique way to enjoy Zhangjiajie’s beauty, the courses here promise an unforgettable experience. In 2024, tee off where nature and sport collide in spectacular fashion.
